## Key Speed Benchmarks to Know

- **172** — Lilligant-Hisui (neutral, max): common Tailwind setter; outspeeding her matters
- **178** — Gengar / Zoroark-Hisui (+spd, max): fast threats that require +spd or Scarf to outspeed
- **184** — Whimsicott (+spd, max): fastest common Tailwind/Prankster user
- **149** — Gholdengo (+spd, max): extremely common; if you can't outspeed, plan for it
- **167** — Palafin / Salamence (+spd, max): Speed tier where many Water/Dragon attackers sit
- **80** — Incineroar (0 EVs): the universal Intimidate benchmark; nearly everything outruns it
- **137–150** — The 85 Base Speed cluster (Rillaboom, Archaludon, Kommo-o, Kingdra): heavy traffic, IV/EV spreads determine ordering
