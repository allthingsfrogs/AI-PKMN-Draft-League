# Wolfey Draft League Rules

## Draft
- The draft is randomized and uses a snake format (1→8, 8→1, repeating).
- Each coach drafts 10 Pokémon with 100 points to spend.
- Pokémon cost between 1 and 20 points based on their tier.

## Free Agency and Trades
- Each team gets 6 free agency pickups, available through the end of Week 3.
- Each team gets 6 inter-team trades, available through the end of Week 3.
- After Week 3, there is a 24-hour grace period with unlimited free agency and trades.

## Battles
- Matches must be completed within 1 week.
- Forfeits count as a 2-0 (+4) win/loss. Forfeiting during a match is prohibited. A forfeit during the final week counts as a 2-0 (+8) loss.
- Battles are played at Level 50, best-of-three.
- Teamsheets are closed, but Tera Types must be shared with your opponent at team preview.
- You must bring 6 Pokémon to each battle.
- Standard VGC rules and clauses apply.

## Scoring
- 2-0 win = 3 points
- 2-1 win = 2 points
- 1-2 loss = 1 point
- 0-2 loss = 0 points

## Tiebreakers (in order)
1. Wins
2. Points
3. Differential
4. Head-to-head
5. Strength of schedule

## Playoffs
- Top 4 coaches qualify.
- Seed 1 vs Seed 4, Seed 2 vs Seed 3.
- 6-week regular season before playoffs.
